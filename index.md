I am Thy (__T__), a __Ph.D. student__ at the University of Manchester, supervised by Prof. [Sophia Ananiadou]({{site.sophia_page}}) at the [NaCTeM]({{site.nactem_page}}) group.

My research focuses on exploiting unlabelled data for neural relation extraction.
In general, I'm interested in applying deep learning for natural language processing.

I'm working with [Phong Le]({{site.phong_page}}) on unsupervised and weakly-supervised relation extraction. 

I spent 3 amazing months at [AIRC/AIST]({{site.aist_page}}) in my 2nd year, where I worked with Prof. [Makoto Miwa]({{site.miwa_page}}) on enriching word representations and event extraction. 

Prior to that, I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science from University of Science, HCMC, Vietnam.
